Family-Mon

This game is a fan-game of the popular Japanese franchise: Pokemon™.

In this version, the Pokemon are replaced by my family. The player controls a team of 4 family-mon fighting clones/copies of our two pets: the fish and hamster both in the wild and handled by trainers.
